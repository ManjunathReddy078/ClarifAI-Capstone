Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ClarifAI.

Who can access ClarifAI?

Only whitelisted students and faculty can register; admin is provisioned internally.

How does NLP sentiment classification work?

Each review is processed via explainable sentiment scoring and routed to approval or moderation.

Is my feedback anonymous?

Feedback is protected from peer visibility and governed by role-based access controls.

Can I edit submitted feedback?

Yes, students can update entries while they are in editable states or after request-for-edit.

What is the Knowledge Board?

A curated board where students and faculty share useful resources and practical experiences.

How are faculty insights protected?

Only approved feedback is visible to faculty dashboards, preserving moderation integrity.
